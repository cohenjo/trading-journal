**Title:** Housing/Utilities Category Taxonomy
**Issue:** User gap report — Meniv Rishon water bill couldn't be categorized. No top-level category for housing-related utility bills (water, electricity, gas, home insurance, property tax, HOA, home maintenance).

### Problem Identified

Existing "Utilities & Communications" category was telecom/streaming-focused (internet, mobile, HBO Max, HOT cable). Housing-related utility bills fell into "Other" or required manual resolution:
- Water utilities (Meniv Rishon, Hagihon, Mei Avivim, etc.)
- Electricity (Israel Electric Corporation / IEC)
- Cooking gas (Pazgas, Supergas, Amisragas)
- Home insurance (ביטוח דירה)
- Property tax / Arnona (ארנונה)
- Building HOA / Va'ad Bayit (ועד בית)
- Home maintenance / repairs

### Solution Implemented

Created **new top-level "Housing" category** (slug: `housing`, Hebrew: דיור, color: #795548, icon: home) with **7 subcategories**:
